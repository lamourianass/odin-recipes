Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a rich and comforting Italian dish made with layers of tender pasta sheets, savory meat or vegetable sauce, creamy béchamel, and melted cheese. Each bite offers a perfect balance of flavor—hearty, cheesy, and perfectly seasoned. Whether baked with a classic Bolognese or a vegetarian twist, lasagna is the ultimate crowd-pleaser, ideal for family dinners, cozy nights, or festive gatherings.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
